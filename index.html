1. Introduction

Welcome to Rewind! This Privacy Policy explains how Invictus Reserve ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use the Rewind mobile application (the "App").

Rewind is a nostalgic photo-sharing social network where you can capture and share one photo per day with friends, send sticky note messages, and connect with others through a unique, vintage-inspired experience.

By using Rewind, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

When you create an account and use Rewind, we collect the following information:

  • Email Address - Used for account creation, authentication, and account recovery
  • Username - Your unique identifier on Rewind, publicly visible to other users
  • Display Name - Optional name shown on your profile
  • Bio - Optional text description of yourself
  • Profile Picture - Optional photo you upload to represent your account
  • Photos - Images you capture and upload through the App
  • Captions - Text descriptions you add to your photos
  • Comments - Comments you post on other users' photos
  • Likes - Your interactions with other users' photos
  • Sticky Notes - Personal thoughts and notes you create on your private bulletin board
  • Sticky Messages - Direct messages you send to mutual followers
  • Notification Preferences - Your choices about which notifications you want to receive

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use Rewind, we automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device Push Notification Token - Required to send you push notifications (via Expo)
  • Usage Data - Timestamps of when you post photos, send messages, and interact with content
  • Preferred Posting Time - The hour of day you typically post photos (used to schedule smart notifications)
  • Daily Post Status - Whether you've posted your daily photo (to enforce our one-photo-per-day limit)
  • Message Read Status - Whether you've read sticky messages sent to you

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't collect:

  • Location Data (GPS) - We do not track your physical location
  • Phone Number - We do not require or store your phone number
  • Contacts - We do not access your device's contact list
  • Photo Library - We only access your camera; we do not access existing photos on your device
  • Browsing History - We do not track your web browsing
  • Analytics or Tracking Data - We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 To Provide Core Features

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Allow you to post and view photos
  • Enable social interactions (follows, likes, comments)
  • Deliver sticky note messages between users
  • Enforce the one-photo-per-day posting limit

3.2 To Send Notifications

We send push notifications when:

  • It's time to post your daily Rewind photo (based on your preferred posting time)
  • Someone likes your photo
  • Someone comments on your photo
  • Someone follows you
  • You receive a new sticky message
  • Reminders to send messages to friends you haven't messaged in a while

You can customize which notifications you receive in the App's notification settings.

3.3 To Improve and Maintain the App

  • Ensure technical functionality
  • Prevent abuse, spam, and harmful content
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve user experience

3.4 To Comply with Legal Obligations

  • Respond to legal requests from law enforcement
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect the rights and safety of our users

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 What's Public

The following information is publicly visible to all Rewind users:

  • Your username, display name, and bio
  • Your profile picture
  • Photos you post and their captions
  • Comments you make on photos
  • Likes you give to photos
  • Your list of followers and who you follow

4.2 What's Private

The following information is private and never shared publicly:

  • Your email address
  • Your password (encrypted and never visible to us)
  • Your sticky notes (only visible to you)
  • Your sticky messages (only visible to you and the recipient)
  • Your push notification token
  • Your notification preferences
  • Your preferred posting time

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as:

  • In response to a valid subpoena or court order
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To prevent illegal activity or violations of our Terms of Service

4.5 What We Do NOT Do

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes unrelated to Rewind
  • Share your private messages or sticky notes with anyone

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement the following measures:

  • Encrypted Passwords - Your password is encrypted and never stored in plain text
  • Secure Connections - All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Row Level Security (RLS) - Database policies ensure you can only access your own data
  • Access Controls - Only you can delete your own photos, comments, and sticky notes
  • Mutual Follow Protection - You can only send sticky messages to mutual followers
  • Regular Security Updates - We keep our systems up to date with security patches

Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Data Retention

6.1 While Your Account is Active

We retain your information for as long as your account is active and as necessary to provide you with the App's features.

6.2 When You Delete Your Account

If you delete your account, we will:

  • Permanently delete your profile information
  • Delete all photos you've posted
  • Remove all your comments and likes
  • Delete your sticky notes and sticky messages
  • Remove all follow relationships

Note: Some data may remain in backups for up to 90 days before being permanently deleted.

6.3 When You Delete Individual Content

  • Photos: Deleted immediately from our servers
  • Comments: Deleted immediately
  • Sticky Messages: Deleted for both you and the recipient
  • Sticky Notes: Deleted immediately
